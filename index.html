I'm a designer and technologist. I write about design for Design is Political and Revision Path.

Recently, I've been researching wellbeing across cultures, to understand how restorative practices – the things we do as humans to regain equilibrium when we fall off-balance – are passed on to future generations.

When I'm at work on a design team, I do a few things:

  • I uncover problems to solve by getting to know the people we serve.
  • I sketch ideas and create prototypes of what might help these people.
  • I partner to build software or services so we can learn what works.

See my design portfolio or contact me to learn more. Thanks for stopping by.
