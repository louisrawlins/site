I'm a designer, educator and technologist. I'm editor-in-chief at People First where I work with John Philpin to build a consulting group that helps people and organizations. I also write about technology and society on this website and Design is Political.

Recently, I've been researching wellbeing across cultures, to understand how restorative practices – the things we do as humans to regain equilibrium when we fall off-balance – are passed on to future generations. I founded Bonfire School as a way to share these ideas and connect with the world.

See my writing or contact me to learn more about my work. Thanks for stopping by.
