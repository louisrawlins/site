Why I write

Delivering your message clearly can be a matter of survival for businesses and people alike. I'm here to help you write and edit your story, whether it's documentation for software, an investigative report on local businesses or a personal memoir. Read on and contact me to learn about my services.

Technical writing, editing and layout

I've hired and led software design teams at Kaiser Permanente and Capital One. I've hustled at break-neck speed with startups acquired by Facebook and Yahoo! I'm comfortable talking to anyone—customers, support, software engineers, testers, product managers, executives and designers. Let my experience work for you to get the information you need organized and published on time. I can handle anything from API specifications and white papers, to journey maps and annotated wireframes. Rates start at $70/hour.

Non-fiction writing and editing

I write regularly technology and social impact, as well as health and wellness. My ability to become a subject matter expert in a matter of weeks brings depth to any story. Let me help you focus and share your work to connect with your audience. Rates start at $50/hour.

Creative non-fiction editing

It was hard to believe my life could be the same as anyone else until I got older. When I was a child, it was clear that I didn't fit in. Everyone is still unique but it's wonderful when it becomes clear that we can be a community of unique people rather than feeling outcast. If this resonates with you, I encourage you to contact me.
